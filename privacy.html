Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 5, 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how our book reading application ("App") handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information or user data.

The App does not:

  • Collect personal information such as name, email address, or phone number
  • Track your reading habits or behavior
  • Collect analytics or usage data
  • Use cookies or tracking technologies
  • Require account registration or login
  • Transmit any data to our servers

Data Storage

All data generated by your use of the App is stored exclusively:

Local Storage

  • Books and reading data are stored locally on your device
  • This data remains under your control and is never transmitted to us

iCloud Storage

  • If you enable iCloud sync, your books and reading data are stored in your personal Apple iCloud account
  • iCloud storage is encrypted and protected by Apple's security measures
  • We do not have access to your iCloud data
  • iCloud storage is subject to Apple's Privacy Policy and iCloud Terms of Service

Books and Content

  • The App does not provide any books or copyrighted content
  • You are responsible for all books you import into the App
  • Your books and content remain your property
  • We do not access, view, or have any knowledge of the books you store in the App

Third-Party Services

Apple iCloud

If you choose to enable iCloud sync:

  • Your data is stored in your personal iCloud account
  • Apple's Privacy Policy applies to data stored in iCloud
  • Apple may collect information about your use of iCloud services
  • You can disable iCloud sync at any time through your device settings

Apple App Store

  • Your download and purchase of the App is subject to Apple's Privacy Policy
  • Apple may collect information about your App Store activities
  • We do not receive any personal information from Apple about App downloads or usage

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children. Since we do not collect any data at all, the App can be safely used by users of all ages. Parents and guardians should supervise their children's use of the App to ensure compliance with copyright laws.

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device or in your personal iCloud account:

  • You are responsible for securing your device with a passcode or biometric authentication
  • You are responsible for managing your Apple ID and iCloud account security
  • We recommend enabling device encryption and keeping your iOS updated

Your Rights and Controls

You have complete control over your data:

  • All data is stored on your device or in your iCloud account
  • You can delete the App and all associated data at any time
  • You can enable or disable iCloud sync in your device settings
  • You can manage your iCloud storage through your Apple ID settings

Data Retention

  • Data is retained on your device until you delete the App or manually remove it
  • If iCloud sync is enabled, data is retained in your iCloud account according to your iCloud settings
  • We do not retain any of your data on our servers

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the App
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Providing notice through the App Store, if required

Your continued use of the App after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

International Users

The App can be used worldwide. Since all data is stored locally on your device or in your personal iCloud account, your data remains under your control regardless of your location.

California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident: Since we do not collect, sell, or share any personal information, California privacy laws regarding data collection and sale do not apply to our App.

Do Not Track

Since we do not track users or collect any data, Do Not Track signals do not affect the App's operation.

Contact Information

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please refer to the contact information provided on our Apple App Store listing.

Consent

By using the App, you consent to this Privacy Policy.
